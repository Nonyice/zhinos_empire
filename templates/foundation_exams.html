IELTS - International English Language Testing System

The IELTS is widely recognized for English proficiency assessment, used for studying, working, or migrating to English-speaking countries.

  • Modules: Academic and General Training
  • Sections: Listening, Reading, Writing, Speaking
  • Scoring: Band score from 1 to 9
  • Validity: 2 years
  • Duration: ~2 hours 45 minutes
  • Website: www.ielts.org

TOEFL - Test of English as a Foreign Language

Used by over 11,000 institutions globally, the TOEFL evaluates English proficiency for academic settings.

  • Format: Internet-based (TOEFL iBT)
  • Sections: Reading, Listening, Speaking, Writing
  • Scoring: Total of 120 (30 per section)
  • Validity: 2 years
  • Duration: ~3 hours
  • Website: www.ets.org/toefl

GRE - Graduate Record Examination

The GRE is required for many graduate programs worldwide, assessing verbal reasoning, quantitative skills, and analytical writing.

  • Sections: Verbal, Quantitative, Analytical Writing
  • Scoring: 130–170 (each section)
  • Validity: 5 years
  • Website: www.ets.org/gre
